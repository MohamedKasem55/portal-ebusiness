  Change Mailing Address
  This help page offers you to change the Mailing address of your Credit Card  

When selecting a structure from the list, if the company user has permission for all the accounts belonging to the selected structure, he will be able to access, modify or delete it. Otherwise, if he does not have permission for all accounts, access is denied and a corresponding message is shown.

You can change your mailing address by changing the fields you wish to change

Information Meaning
Profile Number Your Identification Number
Credit card Number The requested Credit Card
Mailing Address The street of your address
ZIP Code The zip code of your area
PO Box The PO Box of your address
Contact Phone The Phone number that you can be contacted on
Alternate Phone Number The Alternative Phone number the bank can reach you on
Fax Number Your fax number
Mobile Number Your mobile number if any
E-mail Address The new e-mail address you want the bank to contact you on
City Region The region of the mailing address
City The city that is located in the region specified above

After filling the information click proceed
