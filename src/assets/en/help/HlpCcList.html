  Credit Card Statement
  The screen lists your Credit Card transactions. It also allows you to print out your statement, to select historical transactions to be displayed, and to look at more detail of individual transactions.  

Information Meaning
Account number Your Current account number
Date The date that the transaction was posted to your account
Description A description of the type of transaction.
Amount The transaction amount, proceeded by plus (+) or minus (-).- = paid out of your account+ = paid into your account
Balance The amount in your account, after the transaction has taken place.

To look at more detail of individual transactions

Click the Date of the transaction you want to look at

To select historical transactions to be displayed

In the Search Criteria section, select Amount Range or Date Range to show what sort of selection you want

- In Date Range select the Start and / or End dates of the range of transactions you want to select, in dd.mm.yyyy format. All dates are in Gregorian. For example, if you want to select all transactions for January and February 2001, the range would be 01.01.01 to 28.02.01.

- In Amount select the upper and / or lower transaction amounts you want to select, and whether they are Debit or Credit amounts. For example, if you want to select all Credit transactions between 1000 and 5000 Riyal, the range would be 1000 to 5000, Credit.

if no values are entered in either, then no selections will apply, and all transactions will be displayed.
