  Snapshot
  This screen summarizes the status of all your accounts with AlRajhi Bank, and gives you information about your previous logins.  

About logins

The Last Logon Date shows when you last successfully logged onto Al Mubasher Internet Banking. The Last Logon Attempt Date shows when you last tried to logon, but were unsuccessful, perhaps due to an incorrect password.
This information is provided as a security feature. If these are not the dates and times when you last used the service, or tried to, then it is possible that someone else may be attempting to access your account information. In this case, we would recommend that you change your password immediately.

Current Accounts

Each current account that you hold with AlRajhi Bank is listed here. For each current account, the following information is shown
Information Meaning
Account Number The reference number that uniquely identifies your account
Account type Current Account
Current balance The current cleared or available balance in your account.
Select function A "drop down" list of functions that can be performed on your Current Account. Selecting one of these functions will take you to the appropriate new screen.
Option A drop-down box that allows you to select either your Remittance Statement or Beneficiary List

Investments

Each Investment or Mutual Fund account that you hold with Al Rajhi Bank is listed here. For each account, the following information is shown.

Information Meaning
Fund name The name of the Mutual Fund.
Unit Price The current valuation of one unit of the Mutual Fund, which will have taken place in the last 7 days
Units The number of units that you hold in this Mutual Fund.
Valuation The current valuation of your holdings in this fund. This will be the number of units multiplied by the Unit Price
